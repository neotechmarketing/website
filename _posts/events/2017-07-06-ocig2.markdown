This was the second virtual meeting for members of the openCypher Implementers Group, with the aim of discussing, agreeing upon or rejecting Cypher language changes proposed via Cypher Improvement Requests (<a href="https://github.com/opencypher/openCypher/issues?q=is%3Aopen+is%3Aissue+label%3ACIR" target="_blank">CIRs</a>) and Improvement Proposals (<a href="{{ site.baseurl }}/cips/" target="_blank">CIPs</a>).

This meeting was dedicated to the topic of extending Cypher with support for expressing Regular Path Queries (RPQs).

{{ page.agenda }}

## Logistics

The second oCIG meeting was held at 15.00 - 16:30 UTC on Thursday, 6 July 2017.
